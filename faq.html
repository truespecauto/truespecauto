Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our inspection process.

Do I need the seller’s permission for an inspection?

Yes, the seller must allow access to the car so the inspector can perform the inspection. Please coordinate with them before booking.

How long does an inspection take?

Usually between 60–90 minutes depending on the car type, condition, and location.

Will I receive a report?

Yes, after the inspection you will receive a detailed independent inspection report through your secure client portal once payment is confirmed.

Do you check for accident damage?

Yes, the inspection includes a rigorous check of the chassis and body panels for signs of structural repairs or previous accident damage.

Can inspections be done at the seller’s location?

Yes, our inspectors are fully mobile and can travel to the car's location within Nairobi and surrounding areas.
